Md Mostafijur Rahman

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the The University of Texas at Austin advised by Prof. Radu Marculescu. I am a member of System Level Design Group (SLD) @UT Austin. My research addresses computational inefficiency, data scarcity, model robustness, and generalizability in AI-driven biomedical imaging by designing efficient and accurate architectures, multi-modal learning systems, generative techniques, and data-efficient robust training methods for 2D/3D segmentation, synthesis, translation, denoising, and classification. By bridging the gap between AI advancements and clinical deployment, my work enables scalable, reliable solutions for real-world healthcare settings. My research accomplishments are evidenced by several impactful publications in top-tier venues, e.g., CVPR (2025, 2024), MIDL (2025, 2023), WACV (2025, 2024, 2023), ICASSAP 2025, etc. In the past, I have interned at National Institutes of Health (NIH) and Robert Bosch Research.

Prior to joining UT Austin, I was a Graduate Student in Software Engineering at University of Dhaka. I was advised by Prof. Mohammad Shoyaib. I have also spent four wonderful years as a faculty (Assistant professor and Lecturer) at University of Barishal. I had also an oppertunity to work as a Senior Software Enginner at Advanced Solution Lab, Samsung Research, Bangladesh.
