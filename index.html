I am Md Mostafijur Rahman, a final year Ph.D. Candidate at The University of Texas at Austin advised by Prof. Radu Marculescu. My CV | GitHub | Google Scholar | LinkedIn. My email address is mostafijur.rahman@utexas.edu. I design and develop efficient, data-efficient, and robust AI systems for biomedical imaging. My research addresses computational inefficiency, data scarcity, model robustness, and generalizability by creating efficient architectures, multi-modal learning systems, generative techniques, and robust training methods for 2D/3D segmentation, synthesis, translation, denoising, and classification. I've developed methods such as: EffiDec3D (CVPR ’25 Highlight), an optimized decoder for efficient 3D medical image segmentation; LoMiX (NeurIPS ’25), a plug-and-play supervision module for stable multi-scale learning; and EMCAD (CVPR ’24), an efficient multi-scale convolutional attention decoder for enhanced segmentation. My industry and research experiences include internships at GE Healthcare, National Institutes of Health (NIH), and Robert Bosch Research, as well as prior roles at Samsung Research and as faculty at the University of Barishal.

♣ I am currently on the job market, looking for tenure-track faculty or research roles. ♣
